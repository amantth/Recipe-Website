Youtube Tutorial Video

Recipe

  1. Preparation: Wash and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. You can mix and match based on your preferences. If using garlic, peel and finely chop it.
  2. Heat the Pan: Place a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add Oil: Once the pan is hot, add a tablespoon of olive oil or vegetable oil. Allow it to heat for a minute.
  4. Sauté Vegetables: Add the chopped vegetables to the pan. Stir them to coat evenly with oil. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and pepper to season the vegetables.
  5. Cooking:Sauté the vegetables for 5-7 minutes or until they are tender but still have a slight crispness. Stir occasionally for even cooking.
